Unit 1 Outcome 1


1.1 - What is the importance of IT in modern buisness?


The purpose of IT in modern business is anything from communication with customers to research and advertising capacity for the business. Technology affects a business’s ability to communicate with customers. Businesses use IT to communicate with customers quickly and clearly. Businesses rely on their websites to advertise to existing customers and to draw in new customers there by increasing their profits. As customers rely more and more on business’s own social media accounts to communicate with them, the business benefits further as the communications would help to create a stronger public image. IT is also used for communication amongst the employees of the businesses and that would make the business more efficient and better able to provide its service.



1.2 Describe the methods, skills and resources needed to complete digital projects successfully


To complete any digital project successfully, you would need a computer or a laptop to work on. You would need to plan and prepare before starting the digital project. Software such as Trello is ideal to use for the planning, and preparation. Depending on what the digital project is you may need to use python language and use Code-pen or visual studio code to run your code. If for example your project is developing a website, you would use HTML to code the contents of the web page and CSS to define the visual representation of the content.

1.4 Describe the risks that might impact digital projects to fail. What causes digital projects to fail and why?


Digital projects can fail for various reasons such as plain human error and technical reasons such as poor internet connections. The Latter issues can be avoided by saving digital projects as you go along on something like the cloud. Issue such as those caused by human error can be avoided by learning and training. Poor planning, unrealistic expectations, inconsistent communication and lack of cohesion between team members are just some of the few examples of human errors that could cause projects to fail.



1.5 Describe how you would go about selecting and using I.T systems and software – How do you select the right kind of technology for the job?

One of the ways you would decide on what kind of technology to use is by first deciding if you will be working from home/Office or working whilst travelling. If for example you were going be working from the office/ home you may use a desktop computer or if you are going to be mobile whilst working, you would opt to use a laptop, tablet or even a mobile phone. The kind of software you would use could also be dependent on that factor as some software would have a slightly different version for mobile phones and tablets to that of the ones that are available for Desktop computers and laptops.
